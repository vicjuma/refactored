FAQ

  • What does the payment status mean?

    There are next payment statuses on StudyDaddy:

    Paid – The student has paid for your work. The money would be available to withdraw after the 40 days hold period.

    Refunded – The student was unsatisfied with the answer and the money was refunded to the student.

    Reserved for order – The student has accepted your proposal and reserved the money on the StudyDaddy account. Once the order is completed, the money would be transferred to your account.

  • What is the service fee and how is it calculated?

    StudyDaddy takes the service commission on each order. Your service fee is up to the number of the successfully completed orders and the feedbacks of your student. The fee starts at 50% per order amount.

  • What are the payout fees?

    Beware of additional payment fees. For example, there is a payment-processing fee (2.5% +$0.25) for every transfer, charged in addition to website fees. This fee reflects the costs our platform has to pay for the transactions made there. However, such fees are normally much lower than those you have to pay when receiving your salary.
